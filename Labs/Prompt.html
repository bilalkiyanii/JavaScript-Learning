Q.10:

Write a program to implement checkout process of a shopping cart system for an e-commerce website. Take input from users, the following:
a. Name of item1
b. Name of item2
c. Price of item 1
d. Price of item 2
e. Ordered quantity of item 1
f. Ordered Quantity of item 2
g. Shipping charges
Compute the total cost. If the total cost is above 2000 PKR , offer them 10% discount & show the receipt in your browser.

A:
var item1 = "Laptop";
var price = 15000;
var item2 = "LED";
var price2 = 35000;
var user = prompt("Please enter required quantity of item1", "item1");
var user2 = prompt("Please enter required quantity of item2", "item2");
var shippingCharges = 250;
var cost = user * price;
var cost2 = user2 * price2;
var total = cost + cost2 + shippingCharges;
var discount = (total / 100) * 10;
document.write("Price of " + item1 + " is " + price);
document.write("Quantity of " + item1 + " is " + user);
document.write("Price of " + item2 + " is " + price2);
document.write("Quantity of " + item2 + " is " + user2);
document.write("Shipping charges is 250");
document.write("Total cost of your order is " + total + " PKR");
document.write("Discount Price is " + (total - discount) + " PKR");
